The opportunity

A well-systematized heating and cooling contractor serving a growing metropolitan county in south-central Wisconsin. Revenue is diversified across service and repair, system replacement, contracted maintenance, and a light-commercial division; roughly 35% of revenue is recurring or semi-recurring. The company runs on modern field-service software with a tenured management layer in place, and holds the state qualifier credentials required to operate — in more than one employee's name.

Highlights

  • Seller's discretionary earnings grew 35% over two fiscal years on 21% revenue growth — operating leverage, not one-time events.
  • 1,400+ contracted maintenance agreements smooth seasonality and feed the replacement pipeline; renewal reported near 88%.
  • Negligible concentration: largest account under 6% of revenue; ~85% residential across a large active customer base.
  • Management layer (service + installation) runs daily operations; the owner's role is defined and transferable.
  • Positioned early for the heat-pump transition, with certified technicians and dual-fuel install experience.

Transaction

Asset sale, offered debt-free, with fleet, equipment, brand, and customer base conveying. Facility available by lease assignment. The owner proposes a 90-day included transition with extended consulting negotiable, and will execute a non-compete. Reason for sale: retirement after 28 years.
